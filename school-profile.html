About Manuel A. Roxas High School

OUR VISION AND MISSION

Manuel A. Roxas High School envisions itself as one of the Division’s serve centers of educational leadership, innovation and achievement and is committed under a tradition of excellence to human formation of every Filipino through relevant and responsive educational programs to enable the students to succeed in life and to contribute to the further development of the country and its people within the next five years.

OUR EDUCATIONAL GOALS

Optimum development of the potentials of the students, teachers, personnel and other school officials through effective and efficient delivery of quality education towards global competitiveness.

Our School Objectives

Free Access

Free access to free quality and relevant education of every child and educators

Enrich Curriculum

Enrich curriculum in all subject areas / all year levels to improve substantially student's performance

Deeper Commitment

Deeper commitment to endless innovation and educational reforms

Maximized participation

Maximized participation in curricular and co-curricular activities

Strengthened partnership

Strengthened partnership between school, parents, NGOs and the community

Improved participation

Improved participation in the implementation of government thrust and programs
