Programs

Our school offers a DepEd-recognized Senior High School (SHS) program that features Academic and Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) strands as well as a Special Science curriculum for selected individuals.

Junior High School

Manuel A. Roxas High School is one of the six schools in Manila to provide the Special Science course for selected individuals at every junior high school level. This program aims to develop mathematical abilities, science skills, and English proficiency of the students through the use of special additional subjects.
